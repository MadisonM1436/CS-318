Palindromes

A palindrome is a word, phrase, number, or other sequence of characters that reads the same forward and backward.

Opposites

Opposites are words or concepts that contrast with each other.

  1. Adjective: opposite or contrary to a previous or normal condition.
  2. Verb: to turn completely about in position or direction.
  3. Noun: something directly contrary to something else.
